I. GUIDELINES ON ICT COMPETITION


General Guidelines for the ICT Competition

  1. Open to all enrolled undergraduate students of the competing SUC.
  2. Each SUC must have one (1) entry. Each team must consist of three (3) members.
  3. Every contest category is presented with guidelines, contest rules and regulations, criteria for judging and contest proceedings. The contest Faculty In-Charge shall meet all coaches and judges prior to the contest.
  4. Registration shall be done via Google Form. Only registered participants are allowed to compete.
  5. Copy of Student ID and Assessment form shall be submitted via Google Form for validation.
  6. Winners shall be declared/announced during the closing program. The winners shall receive a Certificate for Champion, 1st Place and 2nd Place. Non-winners will also receive Certificate of Participation. Coaches shall receive certificate of recognition provided that they are present during the competition.
  7. Any violation from the competition guidelines will be subjected to disqualification.
  8. Open to all enrolled undergraduate students of the competing SUC.
